My bag

I have always liked seeing people's bags on 2000s websites. Sadly I was too young to own anything interesting to show lol, but here we are, 20 years later, fulfilling that dream!

You might notice my bags have small keychains. I want to get bigger purse pals, maybe in the future? I'm not a fan of Labubu, btw.

Speaking of dreams, an OG Cambridge Satchel was definitely one of them! My initial plan was getting the Vintage one, but this one was on sale and I quite like the navy and red combination. It's the 13 inch one, so it doesn't fit a laptop, but the 15 inch one was too big for my taste.

This Kate Spade is the bag you'll see me using most of the time when I don't need to carry my iPad, headphones, or books. I use it when I leave the house quickly and I basically just need my documents, or when I'm travelling. Just a small bag every girl needs. lol

11 out of 10 women in Europe own one of these! It's also very convenient for travelling because it's foldable. I wanted to get a pink one, but it was limited edition. *cries*
