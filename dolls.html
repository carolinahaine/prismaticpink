Dolls

Dolls are pixel art images that might or might not use bases. I started dolling in 2004, stopped completely in 2008, then came back in 2024. You can check my old dolls here.

All bases are linked to their respective authors if their websites are available. If not, I will just include their names (especially in cases where the person posted their bases in forums). If you know the credits of Unknown bases, please, contact me.

You may adopt those in the Adoptable section. Save the images to your own server, and don’t forget to link back to prismatic.pink.
